• Borrower confirms via signature installation and other requirements/agreements, or stipulations have been met to the Borrower's satisfaction.

Final Funding Conditions

A Loan may be Final Funded after the following conditions are satisfied:

1. The Borrower has acknowledged satisfactory completion of the solar photovoltaic project that is the purpose of the Loan.

The Dealer remains not in default of any obligations to Borrower, Originator or Credit Union (except that Originator or Credit Union may waive this requirement at its sole discretion).

Non-Preferred Dealer:

1. The Borrower has acknowledged (i.e., written or verbal via a recorded call) satisfactory completion of the solar photovoltaic project that is the purpose of the Loan; or

2. The Dealer has supplied evidence to Originator of:

   a. interconnection of the solar photovoltaic project (PTO) that is the purpose of the Loan.

Preferred Dealer:

2. The Dealer has supplied evidence to Originator of at least one of the following*:

   A. The Borrower has acknowledged (i.e., written or verbal via a recorded call) satisfactory completion of the solar photovoltaic project that is the purpose of the Loan

   B. Interconnection of the solar photovoltaic project that is the purpose of the Loan (PTO) within 90 days of final funding*

   C. Production Snapshot

      a. Production Snapshot for two weeks (minimum) within 90 days of final funding*

AXIOM will perform one of the following within 3 days of the Final Funding Request:

• The Borrower has acknowledged satisfactory completion via a phone call via recorded line*.

• The Borrower has acknowledged satisfactory completion via approved email address.

• The Borrower has acknowledged satisfactory completion via approved text phone number.

AXIOM will make a minimum of three attempts to contact the Borrower for acknowledgement of satisfactory completion for final funding disbursement with no response or negative response.

*Requires Dealer to provide AXIOM access to their monitoring reporting and has a minimum of ten initial fundings per calendar month.
